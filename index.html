Gotham's Sidekicks: The Robins

Image Description Abilities Notable Aliases
Richard Grayson Richard John “Dick” Grayson is the first Robin and Batman’s original sidekick. Known for his acrobatics and leadership, he eventually steps out of Batman’s shadow to become Nightwing and even takes up the mantle of Batman when needed. Richard is an exceptional acrobat and martial artist, a skilled detective, and a natural leader with a strong moral compass.
  • Robin
  • Nightwing
  • Batman
Jason Todd Jason Peter Todd is the second Robin, infamous for his tragic death at the hands of the Joker. After being resurrected, he becomes the anti-hero Red Hood, using a more brutal approach to justice. Jason is a skilled martial artist, an expert marksman, and has exceptional tactical skills and physical endurance.
  • Robin
  • Red Hood
Tim Drake Timothy Jackson “Tim” Drake is the third Robin and a brilliant detective who deduced Batman’s identity. He is recognized for his intelligence and his commitment to justice. Tim has exceptional detective skills, advanced computer hacking abilities, strategic thinking, and a high level of martial arts proficiency.
  • Robin
  • Red Robin
Damian Wayne Damian Wayne, the son of Bruce Wayne and Talia al Ghul, is the fifth Robin (the fourth being Stephanie Brown). Trained by the League of Assassins, Damian is a skilled and disciplined fighter. Damian excels in swordsmanship, martial arts, stealth, and strategic thinking, making him formidable at a young age.
  • Robin
